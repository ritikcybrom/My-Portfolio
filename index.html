About Me

The performance marketer
who makes numbers move

5+ years growing revenue across 10+ brands simultaneously, from healthcare chains to fitness apps to industrial steel companies.

I'm a Performance Marketing Manager at Bansal Group Pvt. Ltd., where I oversee paid media for 10+ brands across diverse sectors. My work sits at the intersection of creative strategy and data science — building campaigns that don't just reach people, but convert them into paying customers.

My approach is built on attribution clarity, funnel ownership, and relentless optimization. I don't manage campaigns; I engineer revenue systems. Every ₹1 spent is tracked, every audience is tested, and every funnel stage has a hypothesis behind it.

Before Bansal Group, I worked with AISECT India, Toprankers, OptSEO, and Madbox — building the kind of cross-industry instinct that lets me cut CPL for a hospital the same week I'm scaling ROAS for a fitness platform.
